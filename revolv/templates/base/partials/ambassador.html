About the College Fellowship

The Solar Ambassador College Fellowship is a year-long fellowship for college students who want to help a community-serving nonprofit organization near their campus go solar. RE-volv trains Solar Ambassadors in solar policy, community engagement, and project management. In turn, the Solar Ambassadors educate their campus communities about solar energy, develop a deep understanding of the energy sector, produce a tangible reduction in carbon emissions, and join a national network of environmental stewards. This fellowship also provides valuable training for careers in the solar industry.
